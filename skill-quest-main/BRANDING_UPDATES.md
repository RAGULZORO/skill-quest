## Browser Display

When users visit your site:
1. **Browser Tab**: Will show the PREPMASTER favicon in the tab
2. **Bookmarks**: Will display the favicon next to the PREPMASTER title
3. **History**: Will show PREPMASTER with the custom icon
4. **Search Results**: Will display "PREPMASTER - Ace Your Interviews" as the title

## How to Verify

1. **Clear browser cache** (Ctrl+Shift+Delete or Cmd+Shift+Delete)
2. **Refresh the page** (F5 or Cmd+R)
3. Look at the browser tab - you should see the new **PREPMASTER** favicon and title
4. Right-click the favicon and "Save image as..." to see the beautiful SVG design
